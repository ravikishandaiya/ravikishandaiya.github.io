About Me

Hello! I’m Ravi Kishan, a Software Development Engineer with over two years of experience in backend development and deployment. I graduated from the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Nagpur, in July 2021. Currently based in Rajasthan, India, I am passionate about building innovative solutions and contributing to impactful projects.

In my previous role at Happay (Cred), I was responsible for backend development and deployment, enhancing user experiences and optimizing performance. Notable achievements include developing drill-down functionality, granular charts, user session management, and role-based dashboards, which improved user insights and reduced the effort required for information extraction. I also implemented automated email notification logs for AWS Batch and data load Lambdas, optimized APIs to significantly reduce response times, and improved AWS cloud ETL batches to save significant computational resources. Additionally, I developed a concurrent bulk email and SMS sender, greatly improving notification efficiency. I have a strong command of several programming languages, including Python, C/C++, GoLang, HTML, and CSS. My experience spans various libraries and frameworks such as Django, Pandas, NumPy, Pymongo, and Psycopg2. I am proficient with tools like VScode, Docker, and GitHub, and have expertise in databases including PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, and Amazon DocumentDB. Furthermore, I have hands-on experience with AWS services, covering CI/CD, CodePipeline, CodeBuild, CloudWatch, S3, AWS Batch, and AWS Lambda.
